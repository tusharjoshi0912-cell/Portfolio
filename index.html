About

Who I Am

A Psychology graduate specializing in the intersection of human behavior and emerging technology. My work is driven by a fascination with understanding *why* people do what they do, and then using that insight to build intuitive, engaging, and effective digital solutions. A builder at heart, leveraging the power of generative AI to independently move from a complex human-centered problem to a fully deployed application.

My Philosophy

I believe that the most powerful technology is not just functional, but also deeply human. True innovation begins with a genuine curiosity about the end-user's needs, motivations, and unspoken desires.

This philosophy was put into practice during my time as a Google Student Ambassador. Faced with a user engagement challenge, the solution wasn't just a marketing campaign; it was to build a product. By identifying a core student need—the demand for high-quality study notes—I engineered a gamified platform that offered real value. This "value exchange" transformed a simple marketing goal into a strategic product launch, successfully converting a user need into measurable engagement and proving that the best way to capture attention is to first offer a solution.

What I Do

AI & Development

Hands-on experience acting as a project lead, directing generative AI to build and deploy full-stack applications. Developed not through formal courses, but through independent, hands-on exploration, this skillset is driven by a curiosity to deconstruct and apply these powerful new tools. It includes prompt engineering, rapid prototyping, and managing the end-to-end development lifecycle without writing a single line of traditional code.

Strategy & Product

Specializing in the 'why' behind a product. This includes everything from initial product ideation and user needs assessment to designing the gamified funnels and incentive structures that drive user acquisition and engagement.
